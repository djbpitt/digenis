112знамениезнамениѥ
Devgenij proposes to mark his captives with some sort of disfigurement or defacement before releasing them. The type of marking is unclear, but it may have been cut from or burned into their faces or bodies. See Svetozar Koljević, The epic in the making, Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1980, p. 227. Later in the Digenis text the term пѧтьно is used in place of знамениѥ in a simlar context.
